Common Health Problems

Weakness

Poor nutrition and unhealthy eating habits cause weakness.

Stress

Academic pressure and homesickness increase stress levels.

Obesity

Fast food and lack of physical activity lead to obesity.

Sleep Disorders

Late-night mobile usage affects sleep quality.
